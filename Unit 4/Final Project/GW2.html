While overall more limited, there are still multiple ways to counter chomper's burrow in GW2

  • 1. Engineer Sonic Grenade - stuns chomper and forces them out of burrow
  • 2. Soldier ZPG - instakills chomper during swallow
  • 3. Deadbeard Cannon Rodeo, Deadbeard Barrel Blast, Engineer Big Bolt Blaster - all can tank a chomper burrow hit
  • 4. Scientist Warp, Soldier Rocket Jump/Leap, All-Star Sprint Tackle, Super Brainz Heroic Kick, Super Brainz high jump - all forms of instant mobility
  • 5. Engineer Jackhammer, Hover-Goat 3000 double jump & hover, Imp double jump & hover - slower and weaker forms of mobility
  • 6. Super Brainz (guided) Super Ultra Ball. All-Star Imp Punt - Weaker than ZPG but have the potential to kill a chomper thats taken some damage
