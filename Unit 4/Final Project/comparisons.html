List of methods to counter burrow

Wizard Spell Disaster As it summons 6 orbs doing 80 damage each originating from Wizard, timing this ability well will instantly kill a chomper in the swallowing animation, as each orb will spawn inside the chomper and instantly do damage
Wizard Zee-lixr Temporary invincibility will prevent a burrowing chomper from swalling you for one second, giving you extra time to get to safety
Wizard Co-Star You can avoid burrow by using co-star on another zombie
Soldier ZPG Similar to Spell Disaster, ZPG can instakill a zombie during their swallow animation, either while you are being swallowed or while an ally is
Engineer Bullhorns Even a single bullhorn will force chomper to exit burrow. Extremely powerful counter.
Scientist Warp A short-distance teleport that can help you avoid burrow.
Space Cadet Gravity Smash Gravity Smash's elevation boost will prevent a burrowing chomper from swalling you, and lasts long enough to completely avoid chomper's burrow.
Space Cadet Double Jump/Hover Space Cadet's innate ability to double jump and hover provides enough elevation to dodge chomper's burrow
Space Cadet Space Station Space Station is immune to being burrowed by chomper, so quickly transforming can allow you to avoid a chomper and possibly revenge kill them with Crater Maker
