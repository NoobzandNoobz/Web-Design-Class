Fun Facts About Bees

Egypt began widespread organized beekeeping in 2500 BCE.



Over 100 milion years!



The average lifespan of a queen bee is 2-3 years, but some have lived for as long as 5 years!



If temperatures fall to below 50 degrees fahrenheit, bees will rapidly shiver and form a ball around their queen, keeping her at a nice and warm 90-100 degrees fahrenheit!



When a honey bee knows that they are nearing the end of their lifespan, they will leave the hive as to not trouble the other bees with disposing of their corpse.
